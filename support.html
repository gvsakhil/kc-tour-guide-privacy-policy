Have a question, found a bug, or want to suggest a feature? We're happy to help. The fastest way to reach us is by email — we usually reply within 1–2 business days.

When reporting a problem, it helps to include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Frequently asked questions

The app isn't loading content. What should I do?
Check that you have an active internet connection, then close and reopen the app. If the issue continues, try reinstalling the app or email us with details.
How do I report incorrect information about a place?
Email us with the name of the place and what needs correcting. We review and update content regularly.
How do I request deletion of my data?
Send a deletion request to the support email above. See our Privacy Policy for details on what we collect and how it's handled.
How can I suggest a new feature or location?
We'd love to hear it — just send your idea to the support email.
